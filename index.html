Hello

Open your eyes.

Do you see it?

The destruction,

the only way

to move forward.

To acknowledge

Darkness is

in everybody.

You need to

embrace it

in your soul.

World

wide, there is beauty.

All we ever see is

the pain. Is this

to bring peace,

in this broken world?

We are broken

in our souls,

but that is okay.

Acknowledge and

change the course

towards goodness.
